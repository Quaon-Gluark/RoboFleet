Market and Impact

Our project is aimed at organisations engaged in disaster response, emergency preparedness, and technical education. Primary users include search and rescue agencies, emergency teams, humanitarian NGOs, research labs, and educational institutions exploring robotics applications.

The communities most impacted by disasters benefit directly: our robots increase situational awareness, reduce risk to human responders, and allow faster delivery of supplies and data collection in hazardous environments. We carefully considered cost, deployment speed, accessibility, and real-world constraints when designing our systems.

While advanced rescue robots exist commercially, they are often expensive, complex, and primarily aimed at industrial use. Our project stands out by prioritising affordability, simplicity, and educational accessibility, enabling experimentation and hands-on learning without heavy investment.
